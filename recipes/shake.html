Post-workout Protein Shake

A very tasty, nutritious and quick post-workout shake that doesn't require a blender

Ingredients:

  • 200ml of water
  • 200ml of milk or milk substitute
  • 40g of the protein powder of your choice
  • 1 medium banana (mashed)
  • Optional: a pinch of cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Mash banana with fork until smooth
  2. Place water and milk on a blender bottle
  3. Place protein powder and mashed banana on the bottle
  4. Optional: add a pinch of cinnamon for better taste
  5. Shake until smooth
  6. Drink from the bottle or serve in a tall glass
