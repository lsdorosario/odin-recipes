Scrambled Egg Sandwich

One of my go-to packed lunches and the only recipe I can make that involves a pan

Ingredients:

  • 2 whole eggs
  • A small pad of butter
  • 2 slices of bread
  • A slice of ham or bologna
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Put a small pad of butter on a pan (preferably non-stick) on medium-low heat
  2. Crack the eggs on top and whisk gently until firm but not dry
  3. Season to taste after it's done
  4. Place scrambled eggs on top of the bread
  5. Place slice of ham or bologna on top of the eggs
  6. Close and serve! (or pack for later)
  7. Optional: toast the slices of bread before for better texture
